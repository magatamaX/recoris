Privacy policy

TIL Inc. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") abides by the personal information protection policy as described below, according to a mechanism for personal information protection, recognizes and addresses the importance of protecting personal information of all employees and is committed to promoting the thorough protection of personal information.

Management of personal information

Strict management of personal information is achieved through implementation of safety measures, a security system, a development system, and providing complete training to all employees, enabling the Company to keep customers' personal information accurate and up to date, and prevent unauthorized access to personal information that may result in loss, damage, alteration, leakage, etc.

Purpose of using personal information

Personal information that is obtained from customers will be used for sending e-mails and documents as a response from our company, and to provide guidance for work related content and questions.

Disclosure and provision of personal information to third parties is strictly prohibited.

No personal information of customers will be disclosed to any third party unless it falls under any of the following.
・When there is customer consent
・When such information is entrusted by the company to another business in order to perform a service desired by the customer.
・When it is necessary to disclose based on laws and ordinances.

Security measures for personal information

The Company has taken thorough measures to ensure the security, accuracy, and safety of personal information.

Inquiries involving personal information

In the event a request is made to inquire, modify or delete personal information of an individual that is identifiable by said personal information ("the Owner"), the Company will confirm that the identity of the applicant making the requests is the Owner.

Compliance with laws and regulations, norms and review

The Company will comply with all Japanese laws and regulations applicable to personal information possessed and other norms, and will review the contents of this policy as necessary and make all necessary efforts to improve it.
